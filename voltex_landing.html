Refund Policy

Last Updated: 1 January 2024

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused, in original packaging with all accessories
  • Proof of purchase required for all returns
  • Damaged items eligible for return within 48 hours of receipt

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-ordered or specially fabricated parts
  • Electrical items that have been installed or energised
  • Items damaged due to improper use or installation error
  • Items with broken seals or missing serial numbers

3. Refund Process

Approved refunds processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Replacement

For defective items, we offer a direct replacement. If out of stock, a full refund or store credit.

5. Return Shipping

Return shipping borne by customer unless item is defective or incorrectly shipped.

6. How to Initiate a Return

Email support@voltexelectricals.in with your order number. We provide a Return Authorisation Number (RAN) within 24 hours.

Shipping Terms

Last Updated: 1 January 2024

VoltEx Electricals offers reliable, tracked shipping across India.

1. Processing Time

  • In-stock orders before 3:00 PM IST (Mon–Sat) dispatched same day
  • Orders after 3:00 PM dispatched next business day
  • Custom or bulk orders: 2–5 business days processing time

2. Delivery Timeframes

  • Mumbai Metro: 1–2 business days
  • Maharashtra: 2–3 business days
  • Metro Cities: 2–4 business days
  • Rest of India: 4–7 business days
  • Remote / Hill Areas: 7–10 business days

3. Shipping Charges

Free shipping on orders above ₹5,000. Orders below ₹5,000 attract a flat fee of ₹150.

4. Tracking

A tracking number is emailed once your order is dispatched.

5. Damaged in Transit

Photograph the parcel immediately and email us within 48 hours for a replacement or refund.
